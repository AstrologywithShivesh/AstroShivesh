How Kundli Impacts Your Life Path

Posted on June 17, 2025 | By Shivesh Pati Tripathi

In astrology, a Kundli (natal chart) is the mirror of your life. It maps the planetary positions at the time of your birth and reveals insights into your personality, strengths, and possible challenges.

The twelve houses in a Kundli each represent a different aspect of life: relationships, career, finances, health, and more. A detailed analysis can provide remedies to balance negative effects and boost positive influences.
